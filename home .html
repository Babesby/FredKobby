About Fred Kobby

🇬🇭 Ghana's Afrobeat Sensation

Fred Kobby stands as one of Ghana's most compelling voices in contemporary Afrobeat, delivering authentic sounds that bridge traditional African rhythms with modern global appeal. His music embodies the rich cultural heritage of Ghana while speaking to universal themes of love, struggle, and triumph.

Backed by UK-based Ghanaian superstar Fuse ODG, Fred Kobby has established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the African music scene. His distinctive style combines infectious Afrobeat rhythms with thought-provoking lyrics delivered in both English and Twi, creating a sound that resonates across cultural boundaries.

With performances that electrify audiences and recordings that showcase his versatility as both a vocalist and songwriter, Fred Kobby continues to push the boundaries of Afrobeat music, establishing himself alongside the continent's most celebrated artists.
