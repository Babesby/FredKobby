About Fred Kobby

Born and raised in Ghana, Fred Kobby represents the new generation of African artists bringing authentic Afrobeat to the global stage. With the backing of industry legend Fuse ODG, Fred Kobby is crafting a sound that honors his Ghanaian roots while pushing the boundaries of contemporary African music.

His debut single "Pray" showcases his unique ability to blend traditional African rhythms with modern production, creating music that resonates with both local and international audiences. Fred Kobby's mission extends beyond entertainment – he's using his platform to showcase Ghana's rich musical heritage and inspire the next generation of African creatives.

From the vibrant streets of Accra to studios around the world, Fred Kobby is building more than just a music career – he's building a movement that celebrates African excellence and creativity.
